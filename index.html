What are the amenities offered in The Westpark, Mumbai?

The residences offer generously sized 3 and 4-bedroom layouts, where full-height windows, soaring ceilings, and elegant glass-balustrade balconies create a seamless connection to the outdoors. These homes are private sanctuaries: airy, refined, and designed for contemporary living.

Spacious living and dining areas, along with serene master bedrooms, define each home. Select layouts also include a flexible study space, with interiors designed to suit every aspect of life, from work and rest to entertaining.
